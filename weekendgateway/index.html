Top 5 Weekend Gateways For You


  1. Lonavala-Khandala, Maharashtra

    • Famous for: Known for its stunning valleys, waterfalls, and colonial charm, Lonavala-Khandala offers a perfect escape from city life.
    • Best time to visit: Monsoon (July-September) for lush greenery and waterfalls, or Winter (October-February) for pleasant weather.
    • Lonavala-Khandala, a twin hill station near Mumbai, is a perfect weekend escape. Enjoy stunning waterfalls like Lion's Point and Duke's Nose, explore historical caves, or indulge in adventure activities like trekking and paragliding. The charming colonial-era bungalows and lush greenery create a serene atmosphere. Relish delicious local delicacies like chikki and fudge. Whether you seek adventure, relaxation, or a blend of both, Lonavala-Khandala has something to offer everyone.

  1. Udaipur, Rajasthan

    • Famous for: Immerse yourself in the royal heritage of Udaipur, with its majestic palaces, serene lakes, and vibrant culture.
    • Best time to visit: Winter (October-February) for pleasant weather.
    • Udaipur, the City of Lakes, is a perfect weekend getaway. Immerse yourself in royal grandeur with its stunning palaces like City Palace and Lake Palace. Enjoy boat rides on Lake Pichola, explore vibrant bazaars, and savor delicious Rajasthani cuisine. Don't miss the mesmerizing sunset views from Monsoon Palace. Experience the warm hospitality and rich culture of Rajasthan in this enchanting city.

  1. Coorg, Karnataka

    • Famous for: Experience the beauty of coffee plantations, lush greenery, and adventure activities in this hill station.
    • Best time to visit: October-March for pleasant weather
    • Coorg, a picturesque hill station in Karnataka, offers a perfect weekend escape. Immerse yourself in the lush greenery of coffee plantations, explore charming villages, and indulge in local cuisine. Adventure enthusiasts can enjoy trekking, white-water rafting, and wildlife safaris. Relax by cascading waterfalls or visit historic temples. The cool, pleasant weather makes it an ideal getaway throughout the year.

  1. Rishikesh, Uttarakhand

    • Famous for: Seek adventure with river rafting, trekking, and yoga, or find inner peace amidst the holy Ganges.
    • Best time to visit: March-June and September-November for pleasant weather
    • Rishikesh, the adventure capital of India, offers a perfect weekend escape. Experience thrilling white-water rafting, challenging rock climbing, or find inner peace through yoga and meditation. Explore the holy Ganges, visit ancient temples, and indulge in delicious local cuisine. With its vibrant atmosphere and natural beauty, Rishikesh caters to adrenaline junkies and spiritual seekers alike.

  1. McLeodganj, Himachal Pradesh

    • Famous for: Explore the Tibetan culture, enjoy stunning views, and indulge in delicious Tibetan food in this charming hill station
    • Best time to visit: March-June and September-November for pleasant weather.
    • McLeodganj, a serene hill station nestled in the Himalayas, offers a perfect weekend escape. Known as the abode of the Dalai Lama, it boasts a unique blend of Tibetan culture and natural beauty. Explore the bustling Tibetan market, visit monasteries, and indulge in delicious Tibetan cuisine. Trek to Triund for panoramic views or simply relax amidst the tranquil surroundings. McLeodganj promises a rejuvenating and spiritual experience.
